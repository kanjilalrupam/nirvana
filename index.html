About The Event

NIRVANA’ 21, a 3-DAY inter-college fest of Institute of Engineering & Management (IEM), Kolkata organised by Department of Computer Application and Science is here with its 2nd edition. NIRVANA’21 is here with both technical and non-technical events to be held from 5th -8th July, 2021 in Online. We welcome you to be a part of the family and witness the technical extravaganza.
